Create awesome, full feature maintenance mode for your wordpress like a BOSS.

== Description ==

1.  Full feature Maintenance Mode.
2.  Ability to exclude pages from maintenance mode.
3.  Ability to redirect your site to a link.
4.  Create Awesome counter for you maintenance page.
5.  Background image for your maintenance page.
6.  Background color for your maintenance page.
7.  Background video for your maintenance page.
8.  Simple contact form.
9.  Mailchimp integration.
10. Feedburner integration.
11. Social and much more ...

== Installation ==

1. Upload `plugin-name.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Place `<?php do_action('plugin_name_hook'); ?>` in your templates
